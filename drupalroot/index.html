Fellow Cyclists!

Join us on Sunday, May 6th for an open community meeting to launch a grassroots, bicycle advocacy organization in the Bull City. If you care about biking to work, school, or the grocery store, or enjoy biking Durham’s great trails, or taking a long road ride, we want to hear from you.

We’ll look at bike orgs around the country that have transformed cities and towns. And talk about the challenges we cyclists face locally. But also ask questions – how can we collectively transform Durham into the best bike city in the South, and beyond?

Come ready to contribute your ideas and energy!

3-5pm, Sunday, May 6th, 2012
The Trotter Building
410 West Geer Street @ Foster
Lots-o-bike-parking on site!
